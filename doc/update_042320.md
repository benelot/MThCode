#### Question

Can we confirm the synaptic homeostasis hypothesis (|𝑊𝑠𝑙𝑒𝑒𝑝|<|𝑊𝑤𝑎𝑘𝑒|) [1] and how are the different sleep stages involved in this downscaling?
